Chadwick Boseman

"Wakanda Forever"

Chadwick Boseman (1976-2020) was an American actor best known for his role in Marvel's Black Panther.

    Fun Facts
  • Chadwick attended Howard University, where he later received a scholarship to attend a program at Oxford University in England, funded by none other than Denzel Washington.
  • Chadwick's passion was in directing and writing, but took up acting to improve his abilities.
  • Chadwick did not audition for his role in Black Panther, but was selected by Marvel to play the role.
